## 10. Visual Style Direction

### Overall Style
Clean, organic, simple, soft, and inviting.

### Color Palette

- Warm cream: `#F7F1E6`
- Soft white: `#FFFDF8`
- Sage green: `#7F9C72`
- Olive green: `#556B3E`
- Light sand: `#D9C7A3`
- Charcoal text: `#2F2F2A`

### Typography Direction

- Headings: soft serif or playful organic display font
- Body: clean sans-serif
- Use generous spacing, rounded buttons, and soft corners

### Design Notes

- Keep the background warm and light.
- Use the cart photo as the main visual anchor.
- Add subtle organic shapes, leaf details, or hand-drawn lines.
- Avoid clutter. Let the copy breathe.
- Use large photography, simple icons, and short sections.
